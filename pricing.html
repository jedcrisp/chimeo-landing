Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, all plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to get started.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with our service.

Can I get a custom plan?

Yes, we offer custom enterprise plans for organizations with specific needs. Contact our sales team for more information.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade security measures and are SOC 2 compliant. Your data is encrypted and stored securely.
