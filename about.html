About Chimeo

We're on a mission to revolutionize how communities stay connected and informed

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Chimeo was born from a simple observation: schools, churches, town halls, and community organizations were struggling to communicate effectively with their members. Whether it's critical emergency alerts, sports practice cancellations, venue changes, power outages, or everyday announcements - traditional communication methods were slow, unreliable, and often missed the most important people.

We set out to create a powerful push notification alerting system that would ensure every important message reaches its intended audience instantly. From emergency situations to routine updates, we help organizations keep their communities informed, safe, and connected when it matters most.

Our Mission

To empower community organizations with instant, reliable communication tools that keep everyone informed and safe during critical moments.

Our Vision

To be the leading push notification platform that ensures no important message is ever missed, keeping communities connected and informed worldwide.

Our Values

Reliability, community-first approach, instant communication, and safety guide every decision we make.
